I'm currently studying data science and artificial intelligence in Sorbonne Université (Paris). After completing my master degree, I would like to do a PhD in AI.
I'm interested in computer science since I started debugging programs or video games. Indeed, I started really early ( around 8 years old).
Thanks to books, documentaries and conferences about AI, I'm convinced this field is the one I want to work in. The main books that influenced me are "When the machine learns", "The Most Beautiful History of Intelligence" wrote by Yann le Cun, or currently "Deep Learning: A Practitioner's Approach" wrote by Josh Patterson & Adam Gibson.

That's one of the reason that makes me want to work in research after completing my studies.
